The story of Mr. ImMe




Hey Everyone!
It's me... I'm Mr. ImMe

And if im not wrong... you must be YOU, right?

maybe i am all of those thing they said:

the list went on and on...

I was so sad and angry... didn't know how to handle it or be free from it.

...

One day i decided that enough is enough!
I stopped care about what people though of me... it was very hard from the beginning.
But at last I did learn to love myself for who i am!

I was not:
  • Ugly,
  • I was very much "Good Enough" and
  • I really did LOVE my ears


I WAS GREAT! and nothing else!


So back to start and the IMPORTANT thing i want to tell you.


IT DON'T MATTER!


Who you are,
Where you from,
What you look like or not look like!


Becasue we are all UNIQUE and BEAUTIFUL in our own way!
